About Us

SPORTIFY is a sports equipment marketplace that operates an online marketplace for buying and selling new and used sporting goods. The company’s online marketplace connects individual and team buyers and sellers. Selling on SPORTIFY is free and easy. Just snap a photo of your sports equipment, add a description, set your price, and list it. SPORTIFY provides a safer way to buy and sell when you can’t meet in person. SPORTIFY is headquartered in New York, NY.
